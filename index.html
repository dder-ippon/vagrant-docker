Docker

Docker is an open platform for developing, shipping, and running applications. Docker is designed to deliver your applications faster. With Docker you can separate your applications from your infrastructure AND treat your infrastructure like a managed application.

Docker: Brief History

Created by engineers with dotCloud

First implementation was an open source version of their proprietary DSL wrapper on top of Linux Containers (lxc)

The intention of Docker is to have a standard for packing / bundling / shipping application containers in a consistent way

By version 0.9, lxc was replaced with a custom libcontainer library written in the Go language

Vagrant

Vagrant provides easy to configure, reproducible, and portable work environments built on top of industry-standard technology and controlled by a single consistent workflow to help maximize the productivity and flexibility of you and your team.
